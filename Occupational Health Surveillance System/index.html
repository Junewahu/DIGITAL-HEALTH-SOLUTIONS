Occupational Health Dashboard Project

This dashboard was developed as part of my MBChB portfolio to demonstrate the application of medical knowledge in occupational health settings.

Key Features:

  • Real-time monitoring of workplace health metrics
  • Incident tracking and analysis
  • Employee health status overview
  • Compliance tracking with health regulations
  • Preventive health measures monitoring

Medical Relevance:

The dashboard helps identify patterns in occupational illnesses and injuries, enabling proactive interventions to protect worker health.
