CHADS₂ Score for AF

CHADS₂ Score

--
Select criteria

Stroke Risk

  • 0: Low (1.9% per year)
  • 1: Moderate (2.8% per year)
  • 2: High (4.0% per year)
  • ≥3: Very high (≥5.9% per year)

Consider anticoagulation for score ≥2 (or ≥1 in selected patients).
